## 3. Productivity Gains Are Real but Unmeasured

## 4. Cost Exposure Is Emerging

# 5. Strategic Implications

AI is transitioning from optional tool to operational infrastructure.

Boards must now consider:

* Who owns AI governance?
* How is AI risk incorporated into the enterprise risk framework?
* Is AI usage aligned with strategic objectives?
* Is value being measured, or merely assumed?

Failure to address these questions results in unmanaged risk and missed opportunity.

Structured governance converts AI from liability into competitive capability.

# 6. Recommended Board Actions

1. Assign clear executive ownership of AI governance

3. Integrate AI into enterprise risk management
4. Require periodic AI performance and risk reporting
