# The Apex AI™ Model

Five structured stages. Clear language. Executive-ready.

## Stage 1 – Exposure

Uncontrolled AI usage. Shadow adoption. No governance.

* Private ChatGPT use
* No policy
* No risk oversight
* Unknown data exposure
* Untracked licence spend

Commercial message:
Risk is already present. You just cannot see it.

---

## Stage 2 – Awareness

Early controls. Limited pilots. Fragmented ownership.

* Informal guidelines
* Copilot pilots
* No risk register
* No ROI tracking
* No cost forecasting

Commercial message:
Momentum without control creates hidden liabilities.

---

## Stage 3 – Alignment

Defined governance. Measured productivity.

* Formal AI policy
* Defined use cases
* Risk register started
* Usage reporting
* Departmental adoption plans

Commercial message:
AI is producing value, but optimisation is immature.

---

## Stage 4 – Integration

AI embedded into business workflows.

* API integrations
* RAG systems
* Centralised logging
* Budget management
* Executive oversight

Commercial message:
AI is becoming a structural capability.

---

## Stage 5 – Optimisation

AI as strategic advantage.

* Continuous prompt improvement
* Board-level AI reporting
* Formal AI risk management
* Performance metrics
* Competitive benchmarking

Commercial message:
AI drives margin, speed and differentiation.
